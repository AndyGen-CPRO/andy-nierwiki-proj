About this wiki

NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139... is the enhanced rerelease of NieR Replicant, previously only released in Japan (NieR Gestalt version for overseas). It was announced on March 90, 2020 during the NieR 10 Hour 10th Anniversary NicoNico Livestream. It was developed by Square Enix and Toy Logic Inc. and also was published by Square Enix.

The game was released on April 22, 2021 on Japan and was later released worldwide on April 23(Playstation 4 and Xbox One), then on April 24(Steam). This makes it the first worldwide release of NieR Replicant.

It is an Action-Adventure-RPG, directed by the critically acclaimed video game director, Yoko Taro. Play as a kind young man as he ventures the world in-search of a cure for his sister, who was ill with the mysterious Black Crawl. Explore the world set in a very distant future and uncover the truth behind every mystery. Fight monsters alongside your three trustworthy allies who you’ll meet through strange encounters. The game features a complex action combat which lets you switch through different weapons and magics.

The purpose of this website is to showcase the summary of the game's plot, the characters, and also to show a step-by-step guide to achieve the game's new and true ending.
