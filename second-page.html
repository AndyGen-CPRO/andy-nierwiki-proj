Nier

Description

Nier (unofficial name) is the protagonist of NieR Replicant. He is a kind young man who would do anything for his little sister. He is very strong even at a young age. Aside from the regular odd jobs, he also accepts jobs that requires him to fight and kill shades. His raw fighting skills while wielding one-handed swords, later with two-handed swords and spears, combined with the magic that he uses from Grimoire Weiss makes him a very competent fighter.

Despite his appearance of an innocent, pretty, goody two-shoes. He’s already actually went through a lot of things in life before even the beginning of the game. Throughout the game, we can see how all of this reflects to all of his actions, his devotion to his sister, Yonah, and as to how killing seems to only come as a natural thing to him.

Yonah

Description

Yonah is the younger sister of Nier. She suffers from the mysterious disease, Black Scrawl. She is Nier’s every motivation towards his actions throughout the story. She appreciates everything that her brother does for her but her journal tells that she sometimes just wishes he would stay at home more frequently so they could spend more time together. Despite having a frail body, she is courageous enough to go to the Lost Shrine on her own to look for the mythical flower Lunar Tear, which she heard would make them rich. She is always willing to go far despite being weak to lessen her brother’s burdens.

Grimoire Weiss

Description

The very first companion of Nier. He is a magical floating tome who is also the source of Nier’s magical attacks throughout the game. Nier first meets him in the Lost Shrine while trying to save Yonah. He had forgotten everything from his past after Nier hits him too hard on their first encounter. Very grumpy at first, but after experiencing different adventures with Nier and his companions, he starts developing a caring personality as he starts to bond with them. It’s his connection with humanity that he gained that guides his actions throughout the game.

Kaine

Description

The second companion that Nier meets. She is a skilled fighter that wields dual blades. She has a very strong personality and swears at any chance that she gets. Half of her body is possessed by a shade who always whispers to her to be violent. She’s devoted her life into enacting revenge to a giant Shade before she met Nier. She lives just outside of The Aerie, a village that has heavily rejected her ever since she was a child. Despite all of this, she is very caring and protective towards her companions.

Emil

Description

The third and last companion that Nier will meet in the game. A mysterious young boy that first sent a letter to Nier asking for help in his manor. He is cursed with the power that petrifies anything that he looks at. Because of this, he is forced to wear a blindfold, not being able to see anything or anyone. He asked for Nier and his company’s assistance to accompany him to the hidden basement of his manor to learn more about his power. Despite wielding a very powerful power, he never once thought of abusing it. He is always willing to sacrifice himself for the safety of his companions, which he heavily treasures.

Devola

Description

The bard of the village. She is known as one of the leaders of the village alongside her younger twin sister, Popola. She is always seen performing the song "Song of the Ancients", a song that she sings in a forgotten language. She performs this either at the village fountain or the tavern. She also takes care of Yonah along with Popola each time Nier is working somewhere faraway. She also seems to be the person that makes some heavy choices for the sake of the peace of the village, which sometimes hurts Nier’s feelings but understands and forgives it as he knows that she is only doing it for the sake of the village.

Popola

Description

The librarian of the village and one of the leaders of the village. She helps everyone who is in need of help and provides them with information the best way she can. She cares a lot about both Nier and Yonah and often cares for the latter every time her brother is working outside of the village. She offers Nier guidance most of the game. She is the one that always provides him the information that pushes the story forward.
